Care Guide

Flower Care Tips

Help your blooms stay fresh longer with these expert tips from our florists.

Trim Stems Daily

Cut stems at a 45-degree angle
every 1-2 days to help flowers
drink water more effectively and
stay fresh longer.

Change Water Regularly

Replace vase water every 2 days with fresh, lukewarm water to prevent bacterial build-up that shortens flower life.

Avoid Direct Heat

Keep your flowers away from direct sunlight, radiators, and draughts. Flowers thrive in cool, well-ventilated spaces.

Remove Lower Leaves

Strip any leaves that would be
submerged in water, as they
rot quickly and contaminate
the water.

Use Flower Food

Always use the flower food sachet provided with your bouquet - it contains nutrients and antibacterial agents.

Inspect Regularly

Remove any wilted petals or blooms promptly to prevent them from releasing ethylene gas that speeds up ageing.
