FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about our service.

What is the cutoff time for same-day delivery?
Orders must be placed before 2 PM Monday-Saturday for same-day delivery. Sunday orders should be placed by 11 AM.
How far in advance can I order?
You can schedule orders up to 14 days in advance. We recommend booking at least 2-3 days ahead for special occasions like weddings.
What if nobody is home for delivery?
Our driver will attempt to leave flowers with a neighbour or in a safe place. We'll send a photo and note to let you know where they've been left.
Do you offer a freshness guarantee?
Yes. All Bloomora flowers are guaranteed to stay fresh for at least 7 days. If they don't, we'll replace them free of charge.
Is there a delivery charge?
Delivery charges vary by zone. Central London is GBP 5.99, Greater London is GBP 8.99, and South East England is GBP 12.99. Free delivery on orders over GBP 80.
Can I include a personal message?
Yes. Add your personal message in the order form and we'll include a handwritten card with your flowers at no extra charge.
